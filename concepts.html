Key Concepts

Browser Engine

The component that parses HTML/CSS, executes JS, and renders pages (e.g., Blink, WebKit, Gecko).

Interoperability

Vendors, specs, and tests converge on consistent behavior to reduce site breakage.

Privacy Models

Tracking prevention (ITP/ETP) and API-first ads (Privacy Sandbox) aim to balance privacy and funding.

PWAs

Installable web apps with offline capabilities and app-like UX via Service Workers and manifests.

WebAssembly

A portable binary format enabling near-native compute in the browser.

WebGPU

Next-gen GPU access in the web for advanced graphics and compute workloads.

Engine Diversity

Multiple independent implementations keep the platform robust and competitive.
