Privacy, Ads, and Regulation

Privacy Features

  • Safari/WebKit ITP: cookie limits, storage & cache partitioning, anti-fingerprinting.
  • Firefox ETP: blocks known third-party trackers; mitigates bounce/redirect tracking.
  • Chrome Privacy Sandbox: API-based approach (e.g., Topics, Protected Audience) while slowing third-party cookie deprecation amid regulator scrutiny.

Regulatory Landscape

The EU’s Digital Markets Act (DMA) influenced browser UX and engine openness—iOS added browser choice screens and (in the EU) allowed alternate engines in iOS 17.4. PWA policy changes and developer feedback continue to shape “effective compliance.”
